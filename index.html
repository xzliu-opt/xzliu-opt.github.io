About me

I received the B.S. degree in Information and Computing Science from Northwestern Polytechnical University, Xi'an, China, in 2022. At present, I'm pursuing a Ph.D degree in Applied Mathematics at Beihang University, advised by Prof. Yong Xia. I'm interested in optimization and its applications to signal processing and wireless communications.

Research

My research interests include

  • Optimization theory and algorithms

  • Compressive Sensing

  • Line spectral estimation
